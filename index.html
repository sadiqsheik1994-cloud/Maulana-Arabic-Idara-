How It Works

Your Admission Journey

A simple, transparent 4-step process to begin your educational journey with Maulana Arabic Idara.

1

Submit Admission Form

Fill out the online admission form with your details, course selection, and payment proof. Upload required documents securely.

2

Application Review

Our admin team reviews your application, verifies submitted documents, and evaluates your eligibility within 2-3 working days.

3

Reference Number

Upon approval, a unique Reference Number is generated and communicated to you via WhatsApp and your student dashboard.

4

Course Enrollment

Use your Reference Number to complete course enrollment, receive your Student ID, Login credentials, and begin learning!
